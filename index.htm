I. THE CORE PROBLEM: VISUAL NOISE & FABRIC DECAY

Modern fashion is currently in a state of chaos. We call this "Visual Noise." It is the constant bombardment of loud, meaningless graphics and "hype" that expires within weeks. This noise creates a cycle of Fast-Fashion Fatigue, where clothes aren’t designed to last—physically or aesthetically.


II. THE SOLUTION: NOISE SUPPRESSION & UNIVERSAL DESIGN

KÖCE functions as a Noise Suppression System. We strip away the unnecessary until only the "Signal" remains. Our pieces solve the issue of garment longevity through Structural Engineering.
